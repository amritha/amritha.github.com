CISCO QUAD

Relevant Skills:

Interaction Design, Rapid Prototyping (in Flash), Scrum Collaboration

Summary:

I worked at Cisco Systems from July 2010 to September 2011 as an Interaction Designer. The project I designed for was Cisco Quad, an enterprise collaboration platform, and I owned the interaction for the Discussion Forum and My Library features.

My major responsibilities:

  • I created wireframes using Omnigraffle and Adobe Fireworks to illustrate complex interaction flows for my assigned target areas in order to iterate and improve the overall usability of the product.
  • I simultaneously participated in multiple scrum teams as the User Experience Lead to ensure that code implementation matched design specifications.
  • I created high fidelity interaction prototypes based on design specifications using Adobe Flash Catalyst and Flash Builder.

Cisco Quad became known as WebEx Social after I left the company. You can find out more information about the product itself here.
