Miso User Research

Relevant Skills:

Usability Testing, Personas, Focus Groups, Contextual Inquiry

Process:

Typically when I kick off a sprint of user testing, I like to create a research script about the questions I would like to ask in sessions.

While I keep audio recordings and always make sure detailed notes are taken at every session, I like to capture research snippets on post-it notes. I find it the easiest way to look at an overwhelming amount of research data and categorize it into interpretable clusters.

My favorite research project at Miso was called “What Do People Want While They Watch TV.” I got the opportunity to take a step back from the everyday grind and better understand the thought process of a wide variety of TV watchers.
